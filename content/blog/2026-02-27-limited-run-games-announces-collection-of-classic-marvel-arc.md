Marvel fans and retro gaming enthusiasts have a reason to rejoice, as Limited Run Games has recently announced the Marvel Maximum Collection. This exciting bundle brings together six classic Marvel arcade games from the 8 and 16-bit eras, offering a nostalgic trip down memory lane for veteran gamers and an introduction to timeless Marvel gaming experiences for newcomers.

## 🔍 What Happened?
Limited Run Games, a company known for releasing physical versions of digital games, has partnered with Marvel to create the Marvel Maximum Collection. This collection includes six iconic games from the 8 and 16-bit eras, such as X-Men, Spider-Man, and the Punisher. The announcement was met with enthusiasm from both Marvel fans and retro gaming communities, as it provides an opportunity to play these classic games on modern consoles.

The Marvel Maximum Collection will be available on various platforms, including the PlayStation 4, Nintendo Switch, and PC. The collection will feature faithfully emulated versions of the classic games, ensuring that the original experiences are preserved for new generations of gamers. Limited Run Games is also offering a special edition of the collection, which includes additional items such as a poster, soundtrack, and a retrospective booklet.
