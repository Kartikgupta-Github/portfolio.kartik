## 🔍 What Happened?
I started by testing the MacBook Neo with 10 different games, including popular titles like Fortnite, League of Legends, and Overwatch. I also tested some Windows games using Boot Camp, as well as some Switch emulated games using an emulator. The results were surprising, with the MacBook Neo handling most of the games with ease. The laptop's new processor and improved graphics card made a big difference, allowing for smooth gameplay and fast loading times. However, I did notice some lag and frame rate issues with more demanding games like Assassin's Creed Odyssey and The Witcher 3.
