The Game Developers Conference (GDC) Festival of Gaming has just wrapped up, and the numbers are in: 20,000 attendees flocked to the week-long showcase, which took place from March 9 to March 13. The rebranded event brought together game developers, industry experts, and gamers from around the world to celebrate the latest and greatest in the world of gaming.

## 🔍 What Happened?
The GDC Festival of Gaming was a massive event that featured keynote speakers, panel discussions, and exhibits from top gaming companies. The conference covered a wide range of topics, including game design, programming, art, and audio, as well as the business and marketing side of the industry. Attendees had the opportunity to learn from industry experts, network with peers, and get hands-on experience with the latest games and technologies.

One of the highlights of the event was the keynote speech by industry legend, John Carmack, who talked about the future of virtual reality and its potential to revolutionize the gaming industry. Other notable speakers included game designers, artists, and writers who shared their insights and experiences on creating engaging and immersive gaming experiences.

The event also featured a number of exhibits and demos, showcasing the latest games and technologies from top gaming companies. Attendees had the chance to play upcoming games, try out new hardware and software, and see the latest advancements in fields like artificial intelligence, machine learning, and cloud gaming.
