## 🔍 What Happened?
The development of Kirby Air Riders was a complex and iterative process. Despite these obstacles, the team remained committed to their vision, and their hard work paid off in the form of a game that is both fun and full of depth.

One of the most significant decisions made during the development process was the inclusion of multiple game modes. Sakurai-san explains that the team wanted to create a game that would appeal to a wide range of players, from casual fans to hardcore gamers. To achieve this, they developed several distinct modes, each with its own unique characteristics and challenges. From the high-speed racing of Air Ride to the intense competition of Top Ride, each mode offers a fresh and exciting experience that helps to keep the game feeling new and engaging.
