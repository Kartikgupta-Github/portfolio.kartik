## 🔍 What Happened?
Google's acquisition of Fitbit was announced in 2019, and since then, the company has been working to integrate its AI technology into the device. The plan is to use machine learning algorithms to analyze user data and provide personalized recommendations for improving health and fitness. This will include features such as customized workout plans, nutrition advice, and stress management techniques. Additionally, Google is planning to integrate Fitbit with electronic health records (EHRs), which will allow users to access their medical records directly from the device.

## ⚡ Why This Matters
The integration of AI and access to medical records is a game-changer for the wearables industry. It will allow users to take a more proactive approach to their health and fitness, and will provide healthcare professionals with valuable insights into patient behavior and outcomes. The use of machine learning algorithms will also enable the device to learn and adapt to user behavior over time, providing more accurate and personalized recommendations. Furthermore, the integration with EHRs will streamline the healthcare process, reducing the need for manual data entry and improving the overall patient experience.
